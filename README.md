# Faith-Reality Gap Audit

A public archive of long-form thinking.

This repository documents an ongoing exploration of the growing gap between technological reality and spiritual reality.

Topics include:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Human Identity
- Consciousness and the Soul
- Brain-Computer Interfaces
- Algorithms and Attention
- Digital Power Structures
- Hope, Fear, and the Future
- Competing Narratives of Salvation

The goal is not prediction, but discernment.

Not to reject technology.
Not to worship technology.
But to understand it through a deeper framework of meaning.
